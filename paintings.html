cubo city

This acrylic painting on a 40 x 50 cm canvas interprets contemporary urban life through the lens of Cubo-Futurism. Inspired by Kazimir Malevichâ€™s works and the dynamism of Futurist art, the piece depicts a bustling Belgrade street, where crowded buses, cars, and pedestrians interact in a dense, layered composition. Figures are stylized with geometric and abstract forms, capturing movement and the monotony of city life. Key elements such as traffic lights and vehicle numbers are highlighted with vivid color, while the background features a yellow construction site and repetitive urban buildings, emphasizing the tension between activity and uniformity. The painting reflects the energy, congestion, and mechanical rhythm of the modern urban environment, balancing abstraction with narrative detail.
