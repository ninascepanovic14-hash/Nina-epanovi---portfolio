biogor

This poster, created collaboratively with a small group of colleagues, showcases an experimental custom typeface designed for Hotel Biograd. Key local tourist attractions are presented in this fluid, reflective font across the main body of the poster, while the same names appear in a more legible typeface in the corners for clarity. The typeface’s flowing, distorted forms mimic the movement of light on water, creating a playful, interactive experience where viewers engage with the poster to locate and interpret the names of attractions. The design blends graphic experimentation with functional information, turning the poster into both a visual artwork and an exploratory guide.

bodoni

This project consists of two posters functioning as a diptych, exploring the Bodoni typeface and its striking contrast between thick and thin strokes. One poster highlights the bold, heavy elements of the letters against a dark grey background, while the other emphasizes the delicate, thin lines on a white background. Each composition isolates and magnifies parts of the letters, creating a visual dialogue that showcases the elegance and dynamic tension inherent in the Bodoni font.

poaro

This poster presents the silhouette of Poirot, the seasoned detective, as he navigates a path lined with references to various murders. The red traces in the background subtly suggest the crimes he encounters, reflecting how his life is continually intertwined with solving mysteries. The composition emphasizes his perpetual presence at the scene of the crime, evoking suspense and the inevitability of his investigative journey.

superego

This cover design for Super Ego magazine, dedicated to product design, visually conveys the magazine’s unique sensibility by digitally manipulating the photograph of one of the featured objects. The composition blends graphic design with photography to create a striking, conceptual representation of the magazine’s focus on aesthetics, innovation, and design thinking.

sweet sixteen

Sweet Sixteen is a poster printed on a risograph in yellow and purple, depicting the lively atmosphere of a sixteenth birthday celebration. The composition illustrates the threshold between childhood and maturity, with a crowd of people in the background celebrating, while the flames of the birthday candles reflect the celebrant’s wishes, for family health, togetherness, and guidance in choosing the right path in life. The imagery symbolizes the transition into a period of personal growth and self-discovery, combining emotional depth with playful celebration.

collage self-porter

This self-portrait is a mixed-media collage composed of various papers and illustrations layered to represent different periods of life and facets of personality. The background features a cheerful figure on a yellow backdrop, symbolizing a playful and childlike spirit. Over it lies a dark blue layer depicting a contemplative figure, reflecting introspection and overthinking, while the topmost dark grey layer presents a serious, mature self. The overlapping composition visually narrates the complexity of personal growth, emotional depth, and the multiple selves that coexist within a single individual.
