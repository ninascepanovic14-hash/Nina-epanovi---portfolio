charcoal on kraft paper
portfolio
This series of charcoal drawings on kraft paper explores both figure and still life studies. Each work examines the subtle balance between proportion, form, and light, using the expressive qualities of charcoal to emphasize contrasts between shadow and illumination. The raw texture of kraft paper enhances the depth of the drawings, creating a dialogue between material and subject that reflects the process of careful observation and exploration.
